Uwana Ebong
Director of Studies
Uwana Ebong simply referred to as Bright was born into the family of music lovers. At a very young age, he was exposed to different kinds of music which became part of his muiscal foundation. His love and passion for muisc ministry yielded him different leadership positions in the music ministry and with his skills and God’s grace on his life, he has trained many choirs and individuals not only to sing well but with the fear of God and with skills. Lives has been blessed through hid ministrations and under God, he statred a singing group known as “the psalmist voicees” in 2009. This group ministered in several programmes in and out of Port Harcourt, untill in 2015 when he was led to begim “The psalmist School of Music” due to the needs he saw in the society.
Bright graduated from the University of Port Harcout as a musicologist and did his master’s degree the Ignatius Ajuru University Of Education in Music Education. He is a teacher, a gospel singer, a composer, an arranger and an instrumentalist with The LightHouse Yola.
Adebayo Joyce
Administration/Accounts officer
Joyce is a Secretary and financial coordinator who keeps records, develops all financial accounting systems including payroll and accounts receivable and also reviews all monthly financial reports of the Psalmist School of Music. Joyce has significant experience in different finance administration positions and possess an exceptional ability to establish cooperative, social and professional relationships with parents, staff and administration. In addition to this, she is a dedicated, resourceful and goal-driven educator with a solid commitment to the academic, moral and social growth and development of the students. Obasi Joy
Guitar/violin instructor
Joy Obasi is a music instructor/ composer who teaches beginning to advanced violin ad guitar courses for grade 5 to high school students at the Psalmist School of Music. Joy believes that exposing a child early to music helps in the overall development of the child both intellectually and academically. In addition to managing the violin and guitar curricular for the school, she runs an after school program where he works one-on-one with students. Joy holds a Bsc. in Mass Communication and Postgraduate diploma in Education.
