THE PSALMIST SCHOOL OF MUSIC

The psalmist school of music (PSM) is introduced to the persons who have high regard for quality music education. PSM utilizes curricular and extra-music activities to bring about a perfect blend and completeness in education. The school aims at equalizing music academic work with character building as well as promoting and enhancing natural musical gifts.

Psalmist school of music impacts lives and grooms a generation of musicians who will be dedicated to serve humanity with the fear of God and humility. PSM is open to children and adults.
